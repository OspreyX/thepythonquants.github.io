Senior Advisors

  • Dr Yves Hilpisch

    is Managing Director, Europe of Continuum Analytics Inc., the premier provider of Python-based, enterprise-ready data analytics solutions. He is the founder of Visixion GmbH—The Python Quants, the provider of the first Python-based derivatives analytics suite. He is author of the book Derivatives Analytics with Python, has a doctoral degree in Mathematical Finance and is Lecturer for Mathematical Finance at Saarland University.

  • James Powell

    is an expert Python programmer with extensive experience in Capital Markets. He is a strong supporter of the Python community, and is President of Big Apple Py a foundation established as a 501(c)(3) non-profit whose purpose is the promotion of the Python programming language. Big Apple Py runs NYC Python, the world's largest Python meetup group. James is a frequent speaker and lecturer and has given talks at Python conferences throughout the world. He is also the head organizer of the conference For Python Quants, of the New York Python conference PyGotham, and of the big data conference series PyData

  • Kirat Singh

    previously served as the Head of Global Markets Risk Systems at Bank of America, where he was tasked with building a real-time integrated derivatives and securities trading, risk, analytics, and applications platform. This platform, called Quartz, consists of over ten million lines of Python code written by a team of 4,000 programmers deploying applications to thousands of users' desktops across all lines of business. Quartz is one of the world's largest Python platforms and the largest use of Python in the finance industry. Prior to this, Kirat was at JP Morgan where he led a team which designed and built a global risk and trading system to unified trading, risk, and pricing functions for foreign exchange options, equities, and commodities. Kirat also spent eight years at Goldman Sachs writing and maintaing the core SecDb framework which is used for pricing and risk management by derivatives throughouth the bank.

  • Prof. Jared Lander

    is the Founder and CEO of Lander Analytics a data science consultancy based in New York City, the Organizer of the New York Open Statistical Programming Meetup, and an Adjunct Professor of Statistics at Columbia University. With a masters from Columbia University in statistics and a bachelors from Muhlenberg College in mathematics, he has experience in both academic research and industry. His work for both large and small organizations ranges from music and fund raising to finance and humanitarian relief efforts. He specializes in data management, multilevel models, machine learning, generalized linear models, data management, and statistical computing. He is the author of R for Everyone: Advanced Analytics and Graphics, a book about R Programming geared toward Data Scientists and Non-Statisticians alike.

  • Bryan Wisk

    has over ten years of equity derivatives trading experience both on the sell-side and buy-side. He spent four years as an equity options specialist on the Chicago Board of Options Exchange for Citigroup Global Markets before joining Visium Asset Management in 2006, a long/short equity hedge fund. There he ran equity derivatives trading for the fund. He also worked with the sector PMs on structuring positions with derivatives exposure as well hedging portfolio level risk. At the end of 2011 he left Visium to join Sabretooth Capital Management as a derivatives analyst until the fund closed in April 2012. He graduated with a B.A. in Social Psychology from Yale University in 2001.
